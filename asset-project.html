Asset Management Digital Transformation

Transforming Asset Management services into fully automated workflows through Enterprise Service Management to improve operational efficiency, accessibility, tracking accuracy, and reporting.

Project Overview

This initiative focused on redesigning and automating Asset Management services at SAMI-AEC using Enterprise Service Management (ESM). The project streamlined operational workflows, reduced manual effort, improved request processing, and enabled real-time visibility.

Key Achievements

  • Reduced processing time by 50%+
  • Improved asset tracking visibility
  • Automated approval workflows
  • Enhanced reporting & analytics

Technologies & Tools

ESM Power BI Asset Management Automation Data Analytics

Challenges

One of the biggest challenges was transitioning from traditional manual operations into scalable digital workflows while maintaining operational accuracy and user adoption.

Before & After

Before

Manual Process

Asset Management requests were handled manually, causing delays, limited visibility, and inconsistent tracking.

After

Automated Workflow

Requests became automated through ESM workflows with faster approvals, better tracking, and real-time reporting.
